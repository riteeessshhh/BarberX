The Community

Barber X is a neighborhood barbershop. Always has been, always will be. As we evolve and grow our community, we continue to take a block-by-block approach to everything we do. Our neighborhood roots inform and inspire our spaces, products and practices. Our community sets us apart.


Local design touches reflect the unique style of each community. But the real spirit of each location comes from the barbers, bartenders and customers who call Barber X a second home. Every location feels like Barber X, and they all have a barbershop but check them all out; no two are exactly the same (Other than the drink that comes with your cut and some good times).
